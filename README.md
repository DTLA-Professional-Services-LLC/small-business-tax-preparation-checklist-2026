**Provided by Key Forecasts | DTLA Professional Services, LLC**

To ensure a smooth tax filing and audit process for your business, please prepare the following financial records.

### 1. Financial Statements
* [ ] **Balance Sheet:** Reconciled as of Dec 31st.
* [ ] **Profit & Loss (P&L):** Full year detail.
* [ ] **General Ledger:** Detailed export for the fiscal year.

### 2. Banking & Reconciliation
* [ ] **Bank Statements:** All business accounts (Jan - Dec).
* [ ] **Credit Card Statements:** All business cards (Jan - Dec).
* [ ] **Loan Statements:** End-of-year balances for all liabilities.

### 3. Payroll & Contractors
* [ ] **W-3 & W-2 Summaries:** For all employees.
* [ ] **Form 940/941:** Quarterly payroll tax returns.
* [ ] **1099-NEC:** Copies of forms issued to contractors.

### 4. Asset Management
* [ ] **Fixed Asset Log:** List of equipment/machinery purchased over $2,500.
* [ ] **Inventory Count:** Final value of inventory as of Dec 31st.
